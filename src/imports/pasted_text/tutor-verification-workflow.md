Design a highly modern, premium, minimal verification workflow screen for an Ed-Tech tutor onboarding platform called “Tutor Onboarder”. This is an enterprise SaaS product used by institution administrators to manually verify tutor/candidate information and documents before onboarding them into the Employees tab.

The overall experience must feel:

* Extremely clean and easy to scan
* Calm and low cognitive load
* Highly intuitive for enterprise users
* Premium and modern, not legacy SaaS
* Spacious with excellent visual hierarchy
* Designed with progressive disclosure
* Smooth and engaging with ultra-refined transitions and hover states
* Minimal but visually rich through spacing, typography, surfaces, hierarchy, and motion
* Enterprise-grade yet elegant

Use modern dashboard design patterns similar to Linear, Notion, Stripe Dashboard, Ramp, Attio, Arc, and modern B2B SaaS platforms.

Do NOT create a cluttered admin dashboard.
